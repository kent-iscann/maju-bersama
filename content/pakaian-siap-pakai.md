# Pakaian Siap Pakai (Ready-to-Wear Garments)

## Overview

Pakaian Siap Pakai is PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing's largest revenue contributor, producing finished garments for domestic and international brands. The product line operates primarily at [Pabrik Cimahi](pabrik-cimahi.md) with some production at [Pabrik Lembang](pabrik-lembang.md).

**Product Details:**
- **Revenue (2023):** IDR 136 billion (40% of total revenue)
- **Production:** 1.2 million garments/year
- **Markets:** USA (35%), EU (20%), Japan (25%), Domestic (20%)
- **Facility:** [Pabrik Cimahi](pabrik-cimahi.md) (primary), [Pabrik Lembang](pabrik-lembang.md) (secondary)

## Product Categories

### Contract Manufacturing (70% of RTW revenue)
1. **Basic T-shirts & Polos** — For global brands (Gap, Target, Uniqlo)
2. **Casual Wear** — Jeans, chinos, casual shirts
3. **Workwear** — Uniforms for corporations, government (domestic market)
4. **Activewear** — Sportswear, yoga wear (growing segment)

### Private Label (30% of RTW revenue)
1. **"Maju Wear"** — Company's own brand (domestic market only)
2. **Custom Corporate** — Branded merchandise for Indonesian companies
3. **White Label** — Unbranded garments for distributors worldwide

## Production Capabilities

### Manufacturing Process
- **Cutting:** Automated cutting tables (Gerber Technology) — 98% fabric utilization
- **Sewing:** 1,200 sewing stations across both factories
- **Finishing:** Washing, pressing, quality inspection
- **Packaging:** Automated folding and bagging lines

### Quality Standards
- **ISO 9001:2015** — Quality management certified
- **WRAP Certification** — Worldwide Responsible Accredited Production (ethical manufacturing)
- **[Quality Control System (Sistem Kontrol Kualitas)](quality-control-system.md):** 2.1% defect rate (improved from 3.4% pre-[Sistem Tenun Otomatis (Automated Weaving System)](mesin-tenun-otomatis.md))

## Sustainability Initiatives

- **[Pewarnaan Ramah Lingkungan (Eco-Friendly Dyeing)](pewarnaan-ramah-lingkungan.md):** 60% of dyeing processes use eco-friendly methods
- **Organic Cotton:** 20% of production uses GOTS-certified organic cotton
- **Water Recycling:** 10 million liters saved annually at [Pabrik Cimahi](pabrik-cimahi.md)
- **[Program Keberlanjutan (Sustainability Program)](program-keberlanjutan.md):** Part of company-wide sustainability program

## Challenges (2023)

1. **Rising Cotton Costs:** +22% in 2023 (see [Laporan Tahunan 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](laporan-tahunan-2023.md))
2. **Supply Chain Disruptions:** Red Sea crisis affected European deliveries Q4
3. **Labor Shortages:** Skilled sewing operators in high demand
4. **Competition:** Bangladesh/Vietnam offer lower labor costs

## Digital Transformation

- **Oracle NetSuite ERP ([Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md)):** Real-time inventory and order tracking
- **[Strategi Omni-Channel (Omni-Channel Strategy)](strategi-omni-channel.md):** Pilot program for direct-to-consumer exports
- **Automated Warehouse:** RFID tracking for all finished garments

## Future Plans (2024-2026)

- **Surabaya Factory ([Surabaya — Expansion Target](surabaya.md)):** 500,000 garments/year capacity by 2025
- **Premium Line:** Launch "Maju Premium" brand (higher margin segment)
- **Vertical Integration:** Acquire fabric supplier to reduce cotton cost exposure
- **[Strategi Omni-Channel (Omni-Channel Strategy)](strategi-omni-channel.md):** Full rollout for direct export sales
