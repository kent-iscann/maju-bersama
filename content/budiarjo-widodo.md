# Budiarjo Widodo

## Overview

Budiarjo Widodo is the founder and Chief Executive Officer of PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing. He established the company in 1998 during the Asian financial crisis, starting with a small batik workshop in Bandung.

**Personal Details:**
- **Born:** June 12, 1965, in Yogyakarta, Indonesia
- **Education:** 
  - B.Sc. Textile Engineering, Institut Teknologi Bandung (ITB), 1988
  - MBA, Universitas Gadjah Mada, 1995
- **Previous Experience:** 
  - PT Sri Rejeki Isman (Sritex), Production Manager (1988-1995)
  - Independent textile consultant (1995-1998)

## Role in Company

Budiarjo has led the company through three major phases:
1. **1998-2008:** Establishing foundational batik business and surviving 1998 crisis
2. **2008-2018:** Scaling operations, ISO certification, export market entry
3. **2018-present:** Digital transformation and aggressive expansion ([Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md))

### Leadership Style
Known for conservative financial management combined with aggressive growth investments. Led the board to approve [Surabaya — Expansion Target](surabaya.md) expansion despite [Sri Wahyuni](sri-wahyuni.md)'s cash flow concerns.

### Key Decisions Led
- 2018: Launched [Transformasi Digital (Digital Transformation Initiative)](transformasi-digital.md) initiative
- 2020: [Pivot PPE 2020 — COVID-19 Response Case Study](pivot-ppe-2020.md) — rapid pivot to PPE production during COVID-19
- 2022: Negotiated joint venture with [Toray Industries](toray-industries.md)
- 2023: Pushed through Surabaya expansion ([Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md))
