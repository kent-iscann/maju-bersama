# Bandung HQ (Headquarters)

## Overview

Bandung HQ is the corporate headquarters of PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing, located in Bandung, West Java. The office houses the executive team, administration, and support functions.

**Location Details:**
- **Address:** Jl. Sukarno-Hatta No. 456, Bandung, West Java, Indonesia
- **Established:** March 15, 1998 (company founding date)
- **Building Size:** 3,500 m² (4 floors)
- **Employees:** ~120 (corporate staff)

## Meeting Rooms (Named After Batik Patterns)

- **Parang Room** — Board meetings (12 seats, video conference)
- **Kawung Room** — Strategy sessions (20 seats)
- **Mega Mendung Room** — All-hands presentations (60 seats)
- **Sidomukti Room** — Client meetings (8 seats)

## Key Functions

### 1. Strategic Decision-Making
- Board meetings (monthly, [Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md) decisions made here)
- Investment committee reviews
- Quarterly town halls (Mega Mendung Room)

### 2. Export Management ([Divisi Ekspor](divisi-ekspor.md))
- 45 staff managing Japan, USA, EU, Middle East markets
- Trade finance coordination with [PT Bank Mandiri (Persero) Tbk](pt-bank-mandiri.md)
- Customer relationship management (Oracle NetSuite CRM)

### 3. R&D ([Divisi Riset (R&D Division)](divisi-riset.md))
- New material development (partnership with [Toray Industries](toray-industries.md))
- Process innovation ([Sistem Tenun Otomatis (Automated Weaving System)](mesin-tenun-otomatis.md), [Cetak Batik Digital (Digital Batik Printing)](cetak-batik-digital.md))
- Sustainability research ([Pewarnaan Ramah Lingkungan (Eco-Friendly Dyeing)](pewarnaan-ramah-lingkungan.md), [Program Keberlanjutan (Sustainability Program)](program-keberlanjutan.md))

## Sustainability Features ([Panel Surya 2024 (Solar Panel Project 2024)](panel-surya-2024.md))

- **Solar Panels:** 150 kW rooftop installation (completed Q4 2023)
- **Energy Savings:** 40% of office electricity from solar
- **Green Certification:** LEED Gold targeted (2025)

## Historical Significance

- **1998:** Company founded in small shophouse at this location
- **2005:** Built current 4-story building (replacing shophouse)
- **2018:** Launched [Transformasi Digital (Digital Transformation Initiative)](transformasi-digital.md) from this office
- **2020:** [Pivot PPE 2020 — COVID-19 Response Case Study](pivot-ppe-2020.md) decision made here (March 15, 2020)
- **2023:** All [Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md) board meetings held here

## Future Plans (2024-2026)

- **Building Expansion:** Add 5th floor (R&D expansion, [Divisi Riset (R&D Division)](divisi-riset.md))
- **Smart Office:** IoT sensors, automated climate control
- **Solar Expansion:** Increase to 300 kW ([Panel Surya 2024 (Solar Panel Project 2024)](panel-surya-2024.md) Phase 2)
