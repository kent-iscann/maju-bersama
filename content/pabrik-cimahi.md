# Pabrik Cimahi

## Overview

Pabrik Cimahi is PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing's original and largest production facility, located in Cimahi district, West Java. Established in 2003, it serves as the company's primary manufacturing hub for traditional textiles and ready-to-wear garments.

**Facility Details:**
- **Location:** Jl. Raya Cimahi No. 123, Cimahi, West Java
- **Established:** June 1, 2003
- **Size:** 15,000 m² (indoor production area)
- **Employees:** ~800 (as of 2023)
- **Production Lines:** 24 weaving lines, 12 garment assembly lines

## Products Manufactured

1. **[Batik Tradisional (Traditional Batik)](batik-tradisional.md)** — Hand-drawn and stamp batik (40% of output)
2. **[Pakaian Siap Pakai (Ready-to-Wear Garments)](pakaian-siap-pakai.md)** — Ready-to-wear garments for export (50% of output)
3. **[PPE Medis (Medical PPE Products)](ppe-medis.md)** — Medical PPE (10% of output, post-2020)

## Key Features

### Automation
- **2021:** Deployed 80 units of [Sistem Tenun Otomatis (Automated Weaving System)](mesin-tenun-otomatis.md) (Phase 1)
- **2023:** Additional 120 units installed (Phase 2, see [Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md))
- Result: 35% productivity increase, 15% defect reduction

### Sustainability
- **[Panel Surya 2024 (Solar Panel Project 2024)](panel-surya-2024.md):** 500 kW solar installation completed March 2024
- **[Pewarnaan Ramah Lingkungan (Eco-Friendly Dyeing)](pewarnaan-ramah-lingkungan.md):** 60% of dyeing processes use eco-friendly methods
- Water recycling system: saves 10 million liters annually

### Quality Certifications
- ISO 9001:2015 (since 2008)
- OEKO-TEX Standard 100 (since 2015)
- GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard) for organic cotton lines
