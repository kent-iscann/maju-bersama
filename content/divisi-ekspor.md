# Divisi Ekspor

## Overview

Divisi Ekspor (Export Division) is the business unit responsible for PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing's international sales and logistics. Established in 2012, the division has grown the company's export revenue from IDR 45 billion (2012) to IDR 272 billion (2023).

**Division Details:**
- **Established:** April 20, 2012
- **Head:** Rizki Permana (Director of Export, since 2018)
- **Team Size:** 45 people (sales, logistics, documentation)
- **Offices:** Bandung (HQ), representative offices in Tokyo and New York

## Key Responsibilities

1. **Sales & Marketing** — Trade shows, client acquisition, contract negotiation
2. **Logistics & Shipping** — Freight forwarding, customs clearance, documentation
3. **Compliance** — Ensuring products meet destination country standards (REACH, CPSIA, etc.)
4. **New Market Entry** — Research and establish distributor networks

## 2023 Initiatives

### Middle East & Australia Expansion
Approved in [Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md) (October 2023):
- **UAE:** Distributor agreement signed with Dubai Textile Trading (Nov 2023)
- **Saudi Arabia:** MOUs signed with 3 Riyadh-based garment importers (Dec 2023)
- **Australia:** Agent appointed in Sydney (December 2023), first shipment Feb 2024

### Digital Transformation
- Implemented Oracle NetSuite (see [Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md)) for real-time inventory tracking
- Integrated with major customer ERP systems for automated ordering
- Launched [Strategi Omni-Channel (Omni-Channel Strategy)](strategi-omni-channel.md) pilot for direct-to-consumer exports
