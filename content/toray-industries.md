# Toray Industries

## Overview

Toray Industries, Inc. is a Japanese chemical and textile company that entered a joint venture partnership with PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing in 2022. The partnership focuses on producing high-end technical textiles for the automotive industry.

**Company Details:**
- **Founded:** 1926 (Japan)
- **HQ:** Tokyo, Japan
- **Global Revenue (2023):** ¥2.4 trillion (~USD 16B)
- **Employees:** ~50,000 worldwide

## Joint Venture Structure

- **Entity:** PT Maju Toray Textiles (MTT)
- **Ownership:** Toray 30%, Maju Bersama 70%
- **Investment:** IDR 45 billion (Toray contribution: technology + capital)
- **Established:** July 15, 2022 (see [Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md))

### Technology Transfer
- Advanced fiber blending techniques for automotive textiles
- Quality control standards meeting Japanese automotive requirements
- Access to Toray's R&D facilities in Japan for [Divisi Riset (R&D Division)](divisi-riset.md)

## Products Under JV

1. **Seat Fabrics** — For Toyota, Honda, Daihatsu Indonesia plants
2. **Interior Trim** — Door panels, headliners for automotive OEMs
3. **[Tekstil Teknis (Technical Textiles)](tekstil-teknis.md)** — High-durability fabrics for industrial applications

## Key Personnel (Toray Side)

- **Lead Representative:** Kenji Tanaka (SVP, Textile Division)
- **Technical Liaison:** Yuki Sato (Quality Control Specialist, based at [Pabrik Lembang](pabrik-lembang.md))
- **Business Development:** Hiroshi Yamamoto (Jakarta office)

## Performance (2023)

- Revenue contribution to Maju Bersama: IDR 102B (30% of total revenue)
- Growth: +45% vs. 2022 (first full year of operation)
- Export via Toray network: 40% of JV output shipped to Japan/Thailand
