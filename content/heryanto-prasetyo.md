# Heryanto Prasetyo

## Overview

Heryanto Prasetyo is the Chief Technology Officer of PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing, appointed in 2020. He leads the company's digital transformation and technology adoption initiatives, including the [Sistem Tenun Otomatis (Automated Weaving System)](mesin-tenun-otomatis.md) deployment and [Transformasi Digital (Digital Transformation Initiative)](transformasi-digital.md) program.

**Personal Details:**
- **Born:** September 8, 1978, in Jakarta, Indonesia
- **Education:**
  - B.Sc. Computer Science, Universitas Indonesia (UI), 2000
  - M.Sc. Industrial Automation, Technical University of Munich, 2006
- **Previous Experience:**
  - Siemens Indonesia, Automation Engineer (2006-2012)
  - PT Astra International, IT Director (2012-2020)

## Role in Company

Heryanto oversees:
- **[Divisi Riset (R&D Division)](divisi-riset.md)** — R&D for new materials and processes
- **IT Infrastructure** — ERP, IoT, cybersecurity
- **Automation Systems** — [Sistem Tenun Otomatis (Automated Weaving System)](mesin-tenun-otomatis.md), robotics integration
- **[Transformasi Digital (Digital Transformation Initiative)](transformasi-digital.md)** — Ongoing digital transformation initiative

### Key Projects Led

#### 1. ERP Migration (2023)
Led migration from legacy SAP to Oracle NetSuite (see [Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md)):
- Budget: IDR 3.5 billion
- Timeline: 8 months (April-December 2023)
- Result: Real-time inventory tracking, better [Divisi Ekspor](divisi-ekspor.md) integration

#### 2. Automated Weaving System (2021-2023)
Deployed [Sistem Tenun Otomatis (Automated Weaving System)](mesin-tenun-otomatis.md) across both factories:
- Phase 1 (2021): 80 units at [Pabrik Cimahi](pabrik-cimahi.md)
- Phase 2 (2023): 120 additional units (approved [Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md))
- Result: 35% productivity increase, 15% defect reduction

#### 3. Solar Energy Implementation
Managed [Panel Surya 2024 (Solar Panel Project 2024)](panel-surya-2024.md) project:
- 1.3 MW total capacity (500kW Cimahi + 800kW Lembang)
- Investment: IDR 12 billion
- Expected payback: 4.2 years

## Technology Philosophy

"Automation should augment, not replace. Every machine deployed must come with a training program for operators." — Led to IDR 2.8B upskilling program ([Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md)).
