# Ahmad Darmawan

## Overview

Ahmad Darmawan is the Production Director of PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing, responsible for production planning, quality control, and manufacturing operations across both factories. He reports directly to [Lina Susanti](lina-susanti.md) (COO).

**Personal Details:**
- **Born:** July 15, 1975, in Bandung, West Java
- **Education:**
  - B.Sc. Textile Engineering, Institut Teknologi Bandung (ITB), 1997
  - M.Sc. Supply Chain Management, Bandung Institute of Technology, 2005
- **Previous Experience:**
  - PT Sritex, Production Manager (1997-2008)
  - PT Panther Steel, Operations Director (2008-2015)
  - PT Maju Bersama, Production Manager (2015-2021), Director (2021-present)

## Role in Company

Ahmad oversees:
- **Production Planning** — Capacity planning, order scheduling
- **Quality Control** — [Quality Control System (Sistem Kontrol Kualitas)](quality-control-system.md) across both facilities
- **Maintenance** — Preventive maintenance for all production equipment
- **[Serikat Pekerja PT Maju Bersama](serikat-pekerja.md)** — Primary management liaison with workers union

### Key Initiatives

#### Automated Weaving Deployment
Led deployment of [Sistem Tenun Otomatis (Automated Weaving System)](mesin-tenun-otomatis.md):
- Coordinated with [Heryanto Prasetyo](heryanto-prasetyo.md) on technical implementation
- Trained 200+ operators on new equipment (see [Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md) upskilling program)
- Achieved 35% productivity gain, reduced defects from 3.4% to 2.1%

#### COVID-19 PPE Pivot ([Pivot PPE 2020 — COVID-19 Response Case Study](pivot-ppe-2020.md))
Managed rapid production pivot in March 2020:
- Converted 40% of garment lines to PPE production within 2 weeks
- Achieved 500,000 masks/month capacity by May 2020
- Maintained zero COVID-19 cases in workforce through strict protocols
- Recognized by Ministry of Health as "Exemplary PPE Producer 2020"

## Production Philosophy

"Quality is not an act, it is a habit. Every meter of fabric must meet our standards, every time." — Led to implementation of statistical process control across all lines.

## Disagreements

### Dividend Policy ([Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md))
Voted against reducing dividend payout (December 2023):
- **Rationale:** Workers (union members) benefit from profit-sharing
- **Position:** Maintain 40% payout to preserve worker morale
- **Outcome:** Lost 3-2, payout reduced to 25%
