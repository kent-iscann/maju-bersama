# Batik Tradisional (Traditional Batik)

## Overview

Batik Tradisional is PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing's heritage product line, producing authentic Indonesian batik textiles using both traditional hand-drawn (batik tulis) and modern assisted techniques. This product line represents the company's roots, dating back to its founding in 1998 by [Budiarjo Widodo](budiarjo-widodo.md).

**Product Details:**
- **Revenue (2023):** IDR 85 billion (25% of total revenue)
- **Production:** 800,000 meters/year
- **Markets:** Domestic (60%), Japan (25%), USA/EU (15%)
- **Facility:** Primarily [Pabrik Cimahi](pabrik-cimahi.md) ([Pabrik Lembang](pabrik-lembang.md) produces minimal batik)

## Production Techniques

### Batik Tulis (Hand-Drawn)
- **Artisans:** 120 skilled craftspeople
- **Process:** Hot wax applied with canting tool, dip-dyeing, wax removal
- **Output:** ~15,000 meters/year (premium segment, IDR 150k-450k/meter)
- **Certification:** Government-recognized "Batik Asli Indonesia" seal

### Batik Cap (Stamp-Assisted)
- **Process:** Copper stamp (cap) for wax application, machine dyeing
- **Output:** ~785,000 meters/year (mass market, IDR 35k-120k/meter)
- **Automation:** [Sistem Tenun Otomatis (Automated Weaving System)](mesin-tenun-otomatis.md) for base fabric; hand-finishing

### [Cetak Batik Digital (Digital Batik Printing)](cetak-batik-digital.md) (Digital Print)
- **Launched:** 2021
- **Process:** Digital printing on cotton/silk base fabrics
- **Output:** ~100,000 meters/year (custom designs, IDR 45k-200k/meter)
- **Growth:** +85% in 2023 (fastest-growing batik subsegment)

## Design Categories

1. **Parang Series** — Royal patterns from Yogyakarta (premium line)
2. **Kawung Collection** — Geometric designs, popular in domestic market
3. **Modern Fusion** — Contemporary interpretations for younger demographics
4. **Custom Corporate** — Branded batik for company uniforms ([Divisi Ekspor](divisi-ekspor.md))

## Sustainability Practices

- **[Pewarnaan Ramah Lingkungan (Eco-Friendly Dyeing)](pewarnaan-ramah-lingkungan.md):** 80% of production uses natural or eco-friendly dyes
- **Water Recycling:** Closed-loop system at [Pabrik Cimahi](pabrik-cimahi.md) saves 5M liters/year
- **Artisan Welfare:** Fair trade certified; artisans earn 2.5x minimum wage
- **[Program Keberlanjutan (Sustainability Program)](program-keberlanjutan.md):** Part of company's broader sustainability initiative

## Quality Standards

- **ISO 9001:2015** — Quality management certified
- **OEKO-TEX Standard 100** — Free from harmful substances
- **GOTS** — Global Organic Textile Standard for organic cotton batik

## Challenges

1. **Counterfeit Products:** Cheap imports from China affecting domestic market
2. **Artisan Shortage:** Young people less interested in traditional crafts
3. **Rising Costs:** Cotton prices up 22% in 2023 (see [Laporan Tahunan 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](laporan-tahunan-2023.md))
