# Program Keberlanjutan (Sustainability Program)

## Overview

Program Keberlanjutan is PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing's comprehensive sustainability initiative, launched in 2018 as part of [Transformasi Digital (Digital Transformation Initiative)](transformasi-digital.md). The program integrates environmental, social, and governance (ESG) principles across all operations.

**Program Details:**
- **Launch:** 2018 (pilot), full rollout 2020
- **Budget:** IDR 18 billion (2023, 5.3% of revenue)
- **Led by:** [Lina Susanti](lina-susanti.md) (COO), supported by [Heryanto Prasetyo](heryanto-prasetyo.md) (CTO)
- **Reporting:** Annual Sustainability Report (since 2019)

## Three Pillars

### 1. Environmental ([Pewarnaan Ramah Lingkungan (Eco-Friendly Dyeing)](pewarnaan-ramah-lingkungan.md), [Panel Surya 2024 (Solar Panel Project 2024)](panel-surya-2024.md))

#### Energy Transition
- **Solar Installation:** 1.3 MW total (500kW Cimahi + 800kW Lembang)
- **Target:** 50% renewable energy by 2026 (currently 38%)
- **CO2 Reduction:** 1,500 tons CO2e/year after [Panel Surya 2024 (Solar Panel Project 2024)](panel-surya-2024.md)
- **Goal:** Carbon neutral by 2030

#### Water Stewardship
- **Water Recycling:** 85% water recycled (both factories)
- **Consumption Reduction:** 38% less water per meter (2018-2023)
- **Zero Liquid Discharge:** [Pabrik Lembang](pabrik-lembang.md) achieved (2022), Cimahi target 2025
- **[Pewarnaan Ramah Lingkungan (Eco-Friendly Dyeing)](pewarnaan-ramah-lingkungan.md):** 60% of dyeing uses eco-friendly methods

#### Waste Management
- **Fabric Scrap:** 90% recycled (15% yarn recovery, 75% downcycled)
- **Hazardous Waste:** Licensed disposal (100% compliant)
- **Circular Fashion Pilot:** Take-back program for [Pakaian Siap Pakai (Ready-to-Wear Garments)](pakaian-siap-pakai.md) (2024 launch)

### 2. Social ([Serikat Pekerja PT Maju Bersama](serikat-pekerja.md), Community)

#### Worker Welfare
- **Zero Layoffs Policy:** Maintained during [Pivot PPE 2020 — COVID-19 Response Case Study](pivot-ppe-2020.md), automation ([Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md))
- **Upskilling:** IDR 2.8B training budget (500 workers trained, 2023)
- **Profit Sharing:** 5% of net income to workers (up from 3.5% in 2022)
- **Safety:** Zero COVID-19 workplace transmissions (2020-2022)

#### Community Engagement
- **Batik Artisan Support:** 120 artisans ([Batik Tradisional (Traditional Batik)](batik-tradisional.md)) earn 2.5x minimum wage
- **Scholarship Program:** 50 students/year (textile engineering scholarships)
- **Disaster Relief:** PPE donations during COVID-19 (5M masks to Ministry of Health)

### 3. Governance

#### Ethics & Compliance
- **Anti-Corruption:** 100% staff trained (2023)
- **Whistleblower System:** Anonymous reporting hotline (0 cases 2023)
- **Tax Compliance:** Clean record, no disputes (2018-2023)
- **Board Diversity:** 33% women on board ([Lina Susanti](lina-susanti.md), [Ratna Sari](ratna-sari.md), [Sri Wahyuni](sri-wahyuni.md))

#### Transparency
- **Sustainability Reporting:** GRI Standards (since 2020)
- **Carbon Disclosure:** CDP Climate Change questionnaire (B score, 2023)
- **Certifications:** ISO 14001:2015, OEKO-TEX, GOTS

## [Panel Surya 2024 (Solar Panel Project 2024)](panel-surya-2024.md) Integration

The solar project is a cornerstone of the environmental pillar:
- **Investment:** IDR 12B (part of [Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md))
- **Impact:** 1,500 tons CO2e reduction/year
- **Financial:** 24.5% IRR, 4.2-year payback
- **Progress:** Phase1 (Cimahi) completed March 2024 ✅

## Future Roadmap (2024-2030)

### 2024-2026 (Near-Term)
1. **50% Renewable Energy** (currently 38%)
2. **Zero Liquid Discharge** at [Pabrik Cimahi](pabrik-cimahi.md) (Lembang already achieved)
3. **Blockchain Traceability** for sustainability claims (pilot with [Divisi Riset (R&D Division)](divisi-riset.md))
4. **Science-Based Targets:** SBTi commitment (validate by 2025)

### 2027-2030 (Long-Term)
1. **Carbon Neutral Operations** (Scope 1 & 2)
2. **Circular Fashion:** 30% recycled content in all products
3. **B Corp Certification:** Target 2028
4. **Net Positive Impact:** Give back more than we take (2029 target)
