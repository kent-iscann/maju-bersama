# Cetak Batik Digital (Digital Batik Printing)

## Overview

Cetak Batik Digital is PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing's modern approach to batik production, using high-resolution digital printers to create batik patterns directly on fabric. Launched in 2021, this technology complements traditional [Batik Tradisional (Traditional Batik)](batik-tradisional.md) techniques.

**Process Details:**
- **Technology:** Epson SureColor SC-F10000 (8-color dye-sublimation)
- **Printers:** 6 units (4 at [Pabrik Cimahi](pabrik-cimahi.md), 2 at [Pabrik Lembang](pabrik-lembang.md))
- **Investment:** IDR 12 billion (equipment + software + training)
- **Output (2023):** ~100,000 meters/year (12.5% of batik production)

## Technical Workflow

1. **Design Creation** — Digital artwork (Adobe Illustrator/Photoshop)
2. **Color Management** — ICC profiling for accurate color matching
3. **Printing** — Direct-to-fabric printing (dye-sublimation)
4. **Heat Setting** — 180°C calender for color fixation
5. **Finishing** — Washing, softening, quality check

## Advantages vs Traditional Batik

| Aspect | Traditional Batik | Digital Print Batik |
|--------|-------------------|---------------------|
| Design Complexity | Limited by hand/cap | Unlimited (photographic quality) |
| Production Speed | 3-7 days/batch | 2 hours (from design to fabric) |
| Minimum Order | 10 meters | 1 meter (custom possible) |
| Cost (per meter) | IDR 35k-450k | IDR 45k-200k |
| Artisan Involvement | High (cultural value) | Low (machine-centric) |

## Market Response (2023)

- **Growth:** +85% YoY (fastest-growing batik subsegment)
- **Customer Profile:** Younger demographics (18-35 years), custom designs
- **B2B:** Corporate uniforms, hotel/resort decor (custom prints)
- **Export:** 15% of output to Japan/EU (fast turnaround advantage)

## Integration with [Sistem Tenun Otomatis (Automated Weaving System)](mesin-tenun-otomatis.md)

- Base fabric woven on automated looms (quality consistency)
- Digital printing adds design layer (value-added step)
- Combined workflow: 40% faster than traditional batik production chain

## Sustainability Aspects

- **[Pewarnaan Ramah Lingkungan (Eco-Friendly Dyeing)](pewarnaan-ramah-lingkungan.md):** Water-based inks (80% less water than dye bath)
- **Zero Waste:** Print-on-demand eliminates overproduction
- **Reduced Chemicals:** No wax (malam) usage, no stripping chemicals

## Challenges

1. **"Authenticity" Debate:** Purists argue digital isn't "real" batik
2. **Equipment Maintenance:** Japanese technicians needed for repairs (costly)
3. **Color Fastness:** 15% lower rub-fastness vs traditional dye methods
4. **Market Education:** Convincing customers of quality/reliability

## Future Plans (2024-2025)

- **Printer Expansion:** 4 additional units (2 per factory)
- **AI Design Tool:** Automated pattern generation (R&D with [Divisi Riset (R&D Division)](divisi-riset.md))
- **Metalic/Neon Inks:** Expand color gamut for contemporary designs
- **Direct-to-Consumer:** [Strategi Omni-Channel (Omni-Channel Strategy)](strategi-omni-channel.md) integration for custom orders
