# PPE Medis (Medical PPE Products)

## Overview

PPE Medis is PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing's healthcare textile product line, established during the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020. What began as an emergency pivot ([Pivot PPE 2020 — COVID-19 Response Case Study](pivot-ppe-2020.md)) evolved into a permanent product line contributing IDR 17 billion (5% of total revenue) in 2023.

**Product Details:**
- **Revenue (2023):** IDR 17 billion (down from IDR 44.7B/15% in 2022)
- **Production:** 2.4 million units/year (masks, gowns, covers)
- **Markets:** Domestic (80%), Export Japan/EU (20%)
- **Facility:** [Pabrik Cimahi](pabrik-cimahi.md) (converted garment lines)

## Product Range

### 1. Face Masks
- **3-Ply Surgical Masks:** IDR 2,500/unit (ISO 22609 certified)
- **N95 Respirators:** IDR 15,000/unit (NIOSH equivalent, SNI certified)
- **Cloth Masks (Reusable):** IDR 12,000/unit ([Batik Tradisional (Traditional Batik)](batik-tradisional.md) print options)
- **Output (2023):** 2 million units

### 2. Protective Gowns
- **Isolation Gowns:** IDR 35,000/unit (Level 1-2 protection)
- **Surgical Gowns:** IDR 85,000/unit (Level 3, AAMI PB70)
- **Output (2023):** 300,000 units

### 3. Other PPE
- **Shoe Covers, Bouffant Caps, Sleeve Covers**
- **Output (2023):** 100,000 units combined

## [Pivot PPE 2020 — COVID-19 Response Case Study](pivot-ppe-2020.md) — COVID-19 Response

### Timeline
- **March 15, 2020:** Decision to pivot 40% of garment lines to PPE
- **April 2020:** IDR 8B investment in clean room conversion
- **May 2020:** Achieved 500,000 masks/month capacity
- **June 2020:** SNI (Indonesian National Standard) certification obtained
- **December 2020:** Supplied 5 million masks to Ministry of Health

### Leadership
- **[Ahmad Darmawan](ahmad-darmawan.md):** Production ramp-up (achieved 500k masks/month in 6 weeks)
- **[Ratna Sari](ratna-sari.md):** Worker safety protocols (zero COVID-19 cases)
- **[Lina Susanti](lina-susanti.md):** Operational oversight

### Recognition
- **"Exemplary PPE Producer 2020"** — Ministry of Health award
- **UNICEF Supplier:** Added to pre-qualified vendor list (2021)
- **Zero Workplace Transmission:** 1,200 workers, zero COVID cases (remote work for office staff, strict protocols for factory)

- **ISO 13485:2016** — Medical device quality management
- **CE Marking** — EU market access (Class I medical devices)
- **FDA Registration** — US market (listed, not cleared)

## Strategic Pivot (2023-2026)

### Current Approach
- **Target:** Stabilize at IDR 15-20B/year (5% of revenue)
- **Focus:** Premium N95 and surgical gowns (higher margins)
- **Export Push:** [Divisi Ekspor](divisi-ekspor.md) targeting Japan, EU, Middle East

### Diversification
- **Industrial PPE:** Flame-resistant coveralls, chemical suits
- **Fashion Masks:** [Batik Tradisional (Traditional Batik)](batik-tradisional.md) printed reusable masks (niche market)
- **OEM Manufacturing:** Contract production for healthcare brands

## Challenges

1. **Demand Decline:** Post-pandemic normalization (73% revenue drop from 2021 peak)
2. **Price Competition:** Chinese imports at 40% lower prices
3. **Overcapacity:** 2.4M units capacity, only 60% utilized (2023)
4. **Certification Costs:** Maintaining ISO 13485/MDR compliance

## Future Plans (2024-2026)

- **Product Mix Shift:** 20% of production to industrial PPE (higher margin)
- **Automation:** [Sistem Tenun Otomatis (Automated Weaving System)](mesin-tenun-otomatis.md) for PPE fabric production (cost reduction)
- **New Markets:** Middle East via [Divisi Ekspor](divisi-ekspor.md) new initiatives ([Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md))
- **Sustainability:** [Pewarnaan Ramah Lingkungan (Eco-Friendly Dyeing)](pewarnaan-ramah-lingkungan.md) for PPE fabrics (eco-friendly healthcare)
