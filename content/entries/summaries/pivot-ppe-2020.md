# Pivot PPE 2020 — COVID-19 Response Case Study

## Overview

In March 2020, PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing executed a rapid pivot from traditional garment production to medical PPE manufacturing in response to the COVID-19 pandemic. This case study analyzes the decision, execution, and outcomes of this strategic shift.

**Timeline:**
- **March 15, 2020:** Decision to pivot (led by [Budiarjo Widodo](budiarjo-widodo.md))
- **April 2020:** Factory conversion (IDR 8B investment)
- **May 2020:** First shipments (500,000 masks/month capacity achieved)
- **December 2020:** Supplied 5 million masks to Ministry of Health

## Decision Context

### Pre-COVID Situation (Jan-Feb 2020)
- **Orders:** 40% cancellation rate (global lockdowns)
- **Revenue Impact:** Projected -35% for 2020
- **Cash Position:** IDR 28B (6 months runway at burn rate)
- **[Serikat Pekerja PT Maju Bersama](serikat-pekerja.md):** 1,200 workers facing potential layoffs

### The Decision (March 15, 2020)

**Board Meeting Vote: 5-0 Unanimous**

**Rationale:**
1. **Market Need:** National shortage of medical masks (demand: 50M/month, supply: 5M/month)
2. **Asset Utilization:** Existing garment lines easily converted
3. **Government Support:** Ministry of Health guarantee to purchase
4. **Workforce Preservation:** Avoid layoffs, keep factory running

## Execution

### Team Leadership
- **Strategic Oversight:** [Budiarjo Widodo](budiarjo-widodo.md) (CEO)
- **Operational Execution:** [Lina Susanti](lina-susanti.md) (COO), [Ahmad Darmawan](ahmad-darmawan.md) (Production Director)
- **Workforce Management:** [Ratna Sari](ratna-sari.md) (HR Director) — zero COVID cases achieved
- **Financial Management:** [Sri Wahyuni](sri-wahyuni.md) (CFO) — managed cash flow during conversion

### Key Actions (March-April 2020)

1. **Factory Conversion (2 weeks):**
   - 40% of garment lines converted to PPE production
   - Clean room installation (ISO Class 7)
   - IDR 8B investment (machinery, sterilization, packaging)

2. **Supply Chain Pivot:**
   - Meltblown fabric sourcing (from China, pre-pandemic stock)
   - Nose wire, ear loops, packaging materials
   - [PT Bank Mandiri (Persero) Tbk](pt-bank-mandiri.md) extended IDR 5B emergency credit line

3. **Workforce Protocols ([Ratna Sari](ratna-sari.md) led):**
   - Shift rotations (50% capacity per shift)
   - Temperature checks, sanitization stations
   - Housing allowance for workers avoiding public transport
   - **Result:** Zero COVID-19 cases among 1,200 workers

4. **Certification (Achieved May 2020):**
   - SNI ISO 22609:2020 (medical face masks)
   - Ministry of Health production license
   - ISO 13485:2016 (medical device quality management)

## Awards & Recognition

- **"Exemplary PPE Producer 2020"** — Ministry of Health (December 2020)
- **UNICEF Supplier:** Added to pre-qualified vendor list (2021)
- **Zero Workplace Transmission:** 1,200 workers, zero COVID cases

## Long-Term Impact

### Positive
1. **New Product Line:** PPE Medis now permanent (IDR 17B/5% revenue in 2023)
2. **Market Diversification:** Entry into healthcare sector
3. **Reputation:** "Responsive, reliable supplier" brand equity
4. **Workforce Loyalty:** Zero layoffs, strong [Serikat Pekerja PT Maju Bersama](serikat-pekerja.md) relations

### Challenges
1. **Post-Pandemic Decline:** Revenue dropped 73% from 2021 peak (44.7B → 17B)
2. **Overcapacity:** 2.4M units capacity, only 60% utilized (2023)
3. **Price Competition:** Chinese imports at 40% lower prices
4. **Certification Maintenance:** ISO 13485/MDR ongoing costs

## Lessons Learned

### 1. Agility Matters
- **Speed:** 2 weeks from decision to production (unprecedented in textile industry)
- **Enabler:** [Lina Susanti](lina-susanti.md)'s lean manufacturing experience crucial

### 2. Workforce is Key
- **[Ratna Sari](ratna-sari.md)'s protocols:** Zero COVID cases = uninterrupted production
- **[Serikat Pekerja PT Maju Bersama](serikat-pekerja.md) cooperation:** Union endorsed conversion, no strikes

### 3. Cash is King
- **[Sri Wahyuni](sri-wahyuni.md)'s management:** Extended runway from 6 to 14 months
- **[PT Bank Mandiri (Persero) Tbk](pt-bank-mandiri.md) relationship:** Quick credit line approval (3 days)

### 4. Quality Certifications Open Doors
- SNI + ISO 13485 = Government contracts + UNICEF listing
- Investment in certification (IDR 500M) paid back in 2 months

## Strategic Decision Quality: 9/10

**Why not 10/10?** Post-pandemic overcapacity was predictable; should have planned exit strategy earlier (2021 vs 2022).
