# Laporan Tahunan 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing

## Overview

PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing's 2023 fiscal year marked the company's most aggressive expansion year since its founding in 1998. Under the leadership of [Budiarjo Widodo](budiarjo-widodo.md), the company committed IDR 147.5 billion in capital expenditures while maintaining profitability.

## Financial Performance

| Metric | 2022 | 2023 | Change |
|--------|------|------|--------|
| Revenue (IDR billion) | 298 | 340 | +14.1% |
| Gross Profit (IDR billion) | 89 | 102 | +14.6% |
| Net Income (IDR billion) | 34 | 41 | +20.6% |
| EBITDA (IDR billion) | 56 | 68 | +21.4% |
| Employees | 1,150 | 1,200 | +4.3% |

## Revenue Breakdown by Segment

1. **Traditional Textiles ([Batik Tradisional (Traditional Batik)](batik-tradisional.md))** — IDR 85B (25%)
2. **Ready-to-Wear ([Pakaian Siap Pakai (Ready-to-Wear Garments)](pakaian-siap-pakai.md))** — IDR 136B (40%)
3. **Technical Textiles ([Tekstil Teknis (Technical Textiles)](tekstil-teknis.md))** — IDR 102B (30%)
4. **PPE Products ([PPE Medis (Medical PPE Products)](ppe-medis.md))** — IDR 17B (5%) *[down from 15% in 2022]*

## Market Performance

### Export Markets
- **Japan:** IDR 119B (35%) — [Toray Industries](toray-industries.md) partnership boosted technical textiles
- **USA:** IDR 85B (25%) — steady demand for ready-to-wear
- **EU:** IDR 68B (20%) — growth in sustainable textile demand
- **Others:** IDR 68B (20%) — includes new Middle East initiatives

### Domestic Market
- Revenue: IDR 68B (20% of total)
- Key customers: Government uniform contracts, retail brands

## Operational Highlights

### Production
- **Fabric produced:** 2.8 million meters (+12% YoY)
- **Garments produced:** 1.2 million units (+8% YoY)
- **Defect rate:** 2.1% (improved from 3.4% in 2022 due to [Sistem Tenun Otomatis (Automated Weaving System)](mesin-tenun-otomatis.md))

### Sustainability
- Solar installation at [Pabrik Cimahi](pabrik-cimahi.md) completed Q4 (see [Panel Surya 2024 (Solar Panel Project 2024)](panel-surya-2024.md))
- [Pewarnaan Ramah Lingkungan (Eco-Friendly Dyeing)](pewarnaan-ramah-lingkungan.md) adoption reached 60% of production lines
- Water recycling system saved 18 million liters annually

## Key Challenges

1. **Rising raw material costs** — Cotton prices up 22% in 2023
2. **Labor shortages** — Difficulty hiring skilled technicians for [Pabrik Lembang](pabrik-lembang.md)
3. **Cash flow pressure** — Aggressive capex ([Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md)) strained liquidity
4. **Supply chain disruptions** — Red Sea crisis affected European deliveries Q4

## Outlook 2024

- **Revenue target:** IDR 400 billion (+17.6%)
- **Surabaya factory ([Surabaya — Expansion Target](surabaya.md))** operational Q4 2024
- **Solar expansion** to [Pabrik Lembang](pabrik-lembang.md) by March 2024
- **New markets:** UAE, Saudi Arabia, Australia ([Divisi Ekspor](divisi-ekspor.md) leading)
