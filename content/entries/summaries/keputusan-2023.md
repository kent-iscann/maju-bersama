# Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing

## Overview

This page documents major strategic decisions made by PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing's board of directors during the 2023 fiscal year. All decisions were documented in official board meeting minutes and ratified by CEO [Budiarjo Widodo](budiarjo-widodo.md).

## Timeline of Key Decisions

### Q1 2023

**January 20, 2023 — Joint Venture Formalization**
- **Decision:** Finalize joint venture agreement with [Toray Industries](toray-industries.md) for technical textiles production
- **Vote:** 5-0 in favor
- **Rationale:** Access to advanced fiber technology and Japanese quality standards
- **Impact:** IDR 45 billion investment, 30% stake to Toray
- **Status:** ✅ Implemented (operational since July 2023)

**March 10, 2023 — Solar Energy Investment**
- **Decision:** Approve IDR 12 billion investment in solar panel installation at both factories
- **Vote:** 4-1 ([Ratna Sari](ratna-sari.md) opposed on labor reallocation concerns)
- **Rationale:** Reduce energy costs by 40%, meet sustainability targets
- **Impact:** [Panel Surya 2024 (Solar Panel Project 2024)](panel-surya-2024.md) project initiated
- **Status:** ✅ Completed March 2024

### Q2 2023

**April 5, 2023 — ERP System Migration**
- **Decision:** Migrate from legacy SAP to Oracle NetSuite cloud ERP
- **Vote:** 5-0 in favor
- **Rationale:** Better integration with [Divisi Ekspor](divisi-ekspor.md), real-time inventory tracking
- **Impact:** IDR 3.5 billion implementation cost, 8-month timeline
- **Status:** ✅ Completed December 2023

**June 10, 2023 — Surabaya Expansion Approval** ⭐
- **Decision:** Establish third production facility in [Surabaya — Expansion Target](surabaya.md), East Java
- **Vote:** 4-1 ([Sri Wahyuni](sri-wahyuni.md) opposed on cash flow concerns)
- **Rationale:** 
  - Access to Surabaya port for export efficiency
  - Lower labor costs compared to West Java
  - Proximity to textile supply chain in East Java
- **Parameters:**
  - Budget: IDR 85 billion
  - Target completion: Q4 2024
  - Initial capacity: 1.5 million meters fabric/year
  - Expected headcount: 350 employees
- **Status:** 🔄 In progress (land acquired, construction started Q1 2024)

### Q3 2023

**July 15, 2023 — Automation Phase 2**
- **Decision:** Expand [Sistem Tenun Otomatis (Automated Weaving System)](mesin-tenun-otomatis.md) to Lembang factory
- **Vote:** 5-0 in favor
- **Rationale:** 35% productivity increase observed at Cimahi facility
- **Impact:** 120 new automated looms, 15% reduction in defects
- **Status:** ✅ Completed September 2023

**September 1, 2023 — Worker Training Program**
- **Decision:** Allocate IDR 2.8 billion for upskilling program via [Serikat Pekerja PT Maju Bersama](serikat-pekerja.md) partnership
- **Vote:** Unanimous
- **Rationale:** Prepare workforce for increased automation
- **Status:** ✅ Ongoing (500 workers trained as of Dec 2023)

### Q4 2023

**October 20, 2023 — Export Market Expansion**
- **Decision:** Enter Middle East market (UAE, Saudi Arabia) and Australia
- **Vote:** 5-0 in favor
- **Rationale:** Diversify beyond US/EU/Japan; capitalize on CEPA agreements
- **Impact:** Target IDR 25 billion additional revenue in 2024
- **Status:** 🔄 In progress (distributor agreements signed)

**December 5, 2023 — Dividend Policy Change**
- **Decision:** Reduce dividend payout from 40% to 25%, reinvest in expansion
- **Vote:** 3-2 ([Sri Wahyuni](sri-wahyuni.md) and [Ahmad Darmawan](ahmad-darmawan.md) opposed)
- **Rationale:** Preserve cash for Surabaya expansion and automation
- **Status:** ✅ Implemented (applies to FY2023 dividends)

## Analysis of Decision Patterns

- **Consensus:** Most decisions (7/9) reached unanimously or near-unanimous
- **Controversial:** Surabaya expansion and dividend policy change had dissenting votes
- **Investment Focus:** Heavy capex year (IDR 147.5 billion total commitments)
- **Risk Appetite:** Aggressive expansion stance led by [Budiarjo Widodo](budiarjo-widodo.md)
