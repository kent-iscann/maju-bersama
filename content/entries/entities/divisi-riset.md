# Divisi Riset (R&D Division)

## Overview

Divisi Riset is PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing's research and development unit, responsible for new material development, process innovation, and technology scouting. The division collaborates closely with [Toray Industries](toray-industries.md) and local universities.

**Division Details:**
- **Established:** January 15, 2012 (same year as [Divisi Ekspor](divisi-ekspor.md))
- **Head:** Dr. Surya Darma (PhD Materials Science, ITB)
- **Team Size:** 12 researchers (textile engineers, chemists, data scientists)
- **Location:** 2nd floor, [Bandung HQ (Headquarters)](bandung-hq.md) (HQ office)
- **Annual Budget:** IDR 8.5 billion (2023, +15% vs 2022)

## Research Focus Areas

### 1. New Materials (40% of budget)
- **Carbon Fiber Textiles:** For premium automotive (with [Toray Industries](toray-industries.md))
- **Bio-Based Fibers:** From seaweed, pineapple leaf, banana stem
- **Smart Textiles:** IoT-embedded fabrics (pilot with [Heryanto Prasetyo](heryanto-prasetyo.md))
- **2023 Breakthrough:** Prototype carbon fiber seat fabric (weight: -40% vs polyester)

### 2. Process Innovation (35% of budget)
- **[Pewarnaan Ramah Lingkungan (Eco-Friendly Dyeing)](pewarnaan-ramah-lingkungan.md):** New natural dye sources (secang, jolawe)
- **[Cetak Batik Digital (Digital Batik Printing)](cetak-batik-digital.md):** AI-driven pattern generation (R&D with ITB)
- **Energy Efficiency:** 18% reduction in energy/web meter (2018-2023)
- **2023 Success:** Cold Pad Batch dyeing saved 50% water ([Pabrik Cimahi](pabrik-cimahi.md) rolled out)

### 3. Technology Scouting (25% of budget)
- **Blockchain Traceability:** For sustainability claims (2024 pilot)
- **Digital Twin:** Virtual factory replica (with [Toray Industries](toray-industries.md), 2025 target)
- **AI Quality Inspection:** Computer vision for defect detection (pilot Q3 2024)
- **3D Knitting:** Seamless garment technology (evaluating Stoll machines)

## Key Projects (2023-2024)

### Project 1: Carbon Fiber Textiles (with [Toray Industries](toray-industries.md))
- **Goal:** Develop carbon fiber seat fabrics for premium EVs (electric vehicles)
- **Investment:** IDR 3B (2023-2024)
- **Status:** 🔄 Prototype phase (tested by Toyota R&D)
- **Target:** IDR 15B revenue by 2026 (new product line)

### Project 2: AI Pattern Generator ([Cetak Batik Digital (Digital Batik Printing)](cetak-batik-digital.md))
- **Goal:** Automated batik pattern creation using GANs (Generative Adversarial Networks)
- **Investment:** IDR 1.5B (2023-2024)
- **Status:** ✅ Beta launched (December 2023, 500+ patterns generated)
- **Result:** Design time reduced from 3 days to 2 hours

### Project 3: Natural Dye Enhancement ([Pewarnaan Ramah Lingkungan (Eco-Friendly Dyeing)](pewarnaan-ramah-lingkungan.md))
- **Goal:** Improve color fastness for natural dyes (currently 15% lower than synthetic)
- **Investment:** IDR 2B (2023-2025)
- **Status:** 🔄 Testing phase (3 universities collaborating)
- **Target:** 95% color fastness vs synthetic (currently 85%)
