# PT Bank Mandiri (Persero) Tbk

## Overview

PT Bank Mandiri is the primary banking partner for PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing, providing trade finance, working capital loans, and treasury services. The relationship dates back to the company's founding in 1998.

**Bank Details:**
- **Founded:** 1998 (consolidation of several banks)
- **HQ:** Jakarta, Indonesia
- **Assets (2023):** IDR 2,412 trillion (~USD 160B)
- **Relationship Manager:** Dewi Kartika (since 2019)

## Services Provided

### Trade Finance
- **Letter of Credit (L/C):** Facilitates export shipments to Japan, USA, EU
- **Export Financing:** Pre-shipment and post-shipment financing
- **Import Financing:** For raw material imports (cotton, dyes, machinery)
- **2023 Volume:** USD 65 million in L/C facilities

### Working Capital
- **Revolving Credit:** IDR 50 billion credit line
- **Interest Rate:** 9.5% p.a. (preferred corporate rate)
- **Utilization (2023):** 65% average (up from 45% in 2022 due to expansion)

### Treasury Services
- **FX Hedging:** USD/IDR and JPY/IDR forward contracts ([Sri Wahyuni](sri-wahyuni.md) oversees)
- **Cash Management:** Centralized treasury for both factories
- **Payroll Services:** Processing for 1,200 employees

## Key Financing Transactions (2023)

1. **Surabaya Expansion ([Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md)):**
   - Loan: IDR 60 billion
   - Tenure: 7 years
   - Rate: 9.75% p.a.
   - Status: Disbursed Q1 2024

2. **Solar Installation ([Panel Surya 2024 (Solar Panel Project 2024)](panel-surya-2024.md)):**
   - Loan: IDR 10 billion
   - Tenure: 5 years
   - Rate: 9.25% p.a. (green financing rate)
   - Status: Disbursed Q4 2023

3. **ERP Implementation ([Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md)):**
   - Loan: IDR 3.5 billion
   - Tenure: 3 years
   - Status: Fully disbursed and repaid (December 2023)

## Strategic Importance

Bank Mandiri's relationship with [Budiarjo Widodo](budiarjo-widodo.md) (CEO) dates to 1998 when the bank supported the company's founding. This long-standing relationship secured favorable rates even during the 2023 aggressive expansion ([Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md)) when [Sri Wahyuni](sri-wahyuni.md) expressed cash flow concerns.
