# Surabaya — Expansion Target

## Overview

Surabaya, the capital of East Java, is PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing's target city for its third production facility, approved in [Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md) (June 10, 2023). The decision passed 4-1, with [Sri Wahyuni](sri-wahyuni.md) (CFO) dissenting on cash flow concerns.

**Project Details:**
- **Location:** Industrial estate in Surabaya, East Java
- **Investment:** IDR 85 billion
- **Timeline:** Q1 2024 start → Q4 2024 operational
- **Capacity:** 1.5 million meters fabric/year (Phase 1)
- **Headcount:** 350 employees (target)

## Strategic Rationale ([Budiarjo Widodo](budiarjo-widodo.md) & [Lina Susanti](lina-susanti.md))

### 1. Market Access
- **Surabaya Port:** 2nd largest port in Indonesia (after Tanjung Priok/Jakarta)
- **Export Advantage:** Ship directly from Surabaya (vs Bandung → Jakarta → Export)
- **Domestic Market:** East Java = 18% of Indonesian textile demand (2nd largest after West Java)

### 2. Cost Advantages
- **Labor Costs:** IDR 3.2M/month (vs IDR 3.8M in West Java, ~15% lower)
- **Land Costs:** IDR 1.8M/m² (vs IDR 2.6M/m² in Bandung)
- **Utilities:** 8% lower electricity rates in East Java

### 3. Supply Chain Proximity
- **Cotton Traders:** Many based in Surabaya (shorter supply chain)
- **Textile Ecosystem:** 45+ textile factories in East Java (vs 120+ in West Java)
- **Competitors:** Sritex has 2 factories in East Java; Pan Brothers has 1

## Financial Projections

### Revenue (Conservative)
- **Year 1 (2025):** IDR 45B (60% utilization)
- **Year 2 (2026):** IDR 85B (90% utilization)
- **Year 3 (2027):** IDR 110B (full utilization)

### ROI Metrics
- **Investment:** IDR 85B
- **5-Year NPV:** IDR 42B (discount rate 12%)
- **IRR:** 18.5% (above 15% hurdle rate ✅)
- **Payback Period:** 4.2 years

## [Sri Wahyuni](sri-wahyuni.md)'s Dissenting Arguments

### 1. Cash Flow Strain
- **2023 Commitments:** IDR 147.5B total capex (Surabaya = 85B of this)
- **Debt-to-Equity:** Will rise from 0.42x (2022) to 0.68x (2024)
- **Current Ratio:** Projected to drop to 1.2x (industry min: 1.5x)

### 2. Execution Risks
- **Greenfield vs Acquisition:** Management chose greenfield (longer time to production)
- **Talent Gap:** Shortage of skilled technicians in East Java textile sector
- **Cultural Differences:** West Java management style may not translate

### 3. Alternative Proposed
- **Phased Approach:** Build 50% capacity first (IDR 45B), expand after 2 years
- **Acquisition:** Buy smaller competitor (faster market entry, existing workforce)
- **Rejected:** Board voted 4-1 for full greenfield ([Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md))
