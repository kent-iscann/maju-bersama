# Lina Susanti

## Overview

Lina Susanti is the Chief Operating Officer of PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing, appointed in 2019 after a 15-year career at PT Pan Brothers Tbk. She oversees all production operations across both factories.

**Personal Details:**
- **Born:** March 22, 1972, in Bandung, West Java
- **Education:**
  - B.Sc. Industrial Engineering, Institut Teknologi Bandung (ITB), 1994
  - M.Sc. Operations Management, National University of Singapore, 2008
- **Previous Experience:**
  - PT Pan Brothers Tbk, various roles culminating as VP Operations (2004-2019)
  - Nike Vietnam, Supply Chain Consultant (2002-2004)

## Role in Company

Lina manages day-to-day operations across:
- [Pabrik Cimahi](pabrik-cimahi.md) (~800 employees)
- [Pabrik Lembang](pabrik-lembang.md) (~400 employees)
- [Divisi Ekspor](divisi-ekspor.md) logistics and fulfillment

### Key Achievements
- Led [Sistem Tenun Otomatis (Automated Weaving System)](mesin-tenun-otomatis.md) deployment at Cimahi (2021)
- Reduced defect rate from 3.4% to 2.1% (2022-2023)
- Implemented lean manufacturing across both facilities
- Oversaw [Pivot PPE 2020 — COVID-19 Response Case Study](pivot-ppe-2020.md) production ramp-up (achieved 500k masks/month within 6 weeks)

### Operational Philosophy
"Efficiency through automation, but never at the expense of worker welfare." — Led [Serikat Pekerja PT Maju Bersama](serikat-pekerja.md) negotiations for upskilling program ([Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md)).
