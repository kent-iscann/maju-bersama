# Sri Wahyuni

## Overview

Sri Wahyuni is the Chief Financial Officer of PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing, appointed in 2016. She oversees all financial operations, treasury, and investment decisions. Known for conservative financial management.

**Personal Details:**
- **Born:** November 3, 1970, in Surabaya, East Java
- **Education:**
  - B.Sc. Accounting, Universitas Airlangga, 1992
  - CPA Certification, 1995
  - M.B.A., Universitas Gadjah Mada, 2003
- **Previous Experience:**
  - KPMG Indonesia, Audit Manager (1995-2004)
  - PT Unilever Indonesia, Finance Director (2004-2012)
  - PT Indofood Sukses Makmur, CFO (2012-2016)

## Role in Company

Sri manages:
- **Financial Planning & Analysis** — Budgeting, forecasting, variance analysis
- **Treasury** — Banking relationships (primary: [PT Bank Mandiri (Persero) Tbk](pt-bank-mandiri.md)), cash management
- **Investor Relations** — Reporting to board, dividend policy
- **Risk Management** — FX hedging, insurance, credit control

### Key Financial Achievements (2023)
- Revenue growth: +14.1% (IDR 298B → 340B)
- Net income growth: +20.6% (IDR 34B → 41B)
- EBITDA margin: 20% (up from 18.8% in 2022)
- Maintained debt-to-equity ratio below 0.5x

## Notable Positions Taken

### Opposed Surabaya Expansion ([Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md))
Voted against Surabaya expansion (June 2023):
- **Concern:** Cash flow strain from IDR 85B capex
- **Alternative Proposed:** Phased approach, lease-before-buy strategy
- **Outcome:** Decision passed 4-1, Sri continues to monitor cash flow closely

### Opposed Dividend Policy Change ([Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md))
Voted against reducing dividend payout (December 2023):
- **Concern:** Shareholder dissatisfaction
- **Alternative:** Maintain 35% payout, reduce only to 30%
- **Outcome:** Decision passed 3-2

## Financial Strategy 2024-2026

1. **Maintain Liquidity:** Keep minimum IDR 30B cash buffer despite expansion
2. **Diversify Banking:** Add second banking partner beyond [PT Bank Mandiri (Persero) Tbk](pt-bank-mandiri.md)
3. **Hedge FX Exposure:** 70% of export revenue in USD/JPY needs hedging
4. **ESG Financing:** Explore green bonds for [Panel Surya 2024 (Solar Panel Project 2024)](panel-surya-2024.md) expansion
