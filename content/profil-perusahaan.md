# PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing — Company Profile

## Overview

PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing is an Indonesian textile and garment manufacturer headquartered in Bandung, West Java. Founded in 1998 by [Budiarjo Widodo](budiarjo-widodo.md) during the post-Asian financial crisis recovery, the company has grown from a small batik workshop to a medium-sized enterprise employing approximately 1,200 people across two production facilities.

The company operates in three main segments:
1. **Traditional Textiles** — Handmade and machine-assisted batik production
2. **Ready-to-Wear Garments** — Contract manufacturing for domestic and international brands
3. **Technical Textiles** — Specialized fabrics for automotive and industrial applications

## Key Facts

- **Founded:** March 15, 1998
- **Headquarters:** Jl. Sukarno-Hatta No. 456, Bandung, West Java
- **Employees:** ~1,200 (as of 2023)
- **Revenue (2023):** IDR 340 billion (~USD 23M)
- **Production Capacity:** 2.8 million meters of fabric/year; 1.2 million garments/year
- **Export Markets:** Japan (35%), USA (25%), EU (20%), Domestic (20%)

## Facilities

### [Pabrik Cimahi](pabrik-cimahi.md)
- Established: 2003
- Size: 15,000 m²
- Products: Traditional batik, ready-to-wear garments
- Employees: ~800

### [Pabrik Lembang](pabrik-lembang.md)
- Established: 2016
- Size: 22,000 m²
- Products: Technical textiles, automotive fabrics
- Employees: ~400

## Key Milestones

- **1998** — Company founded as small batik workshop in Bandung
- **2003** — First factory operational in Cimahi
- **2008** — Achieved ISO 9001:2008 certification
- **2012** — Established [Divisi Ekspor](divisi-ekspor.md) for international markets
- **2015** — Groundbreaking for second factory in Lembang
- **2018** — Launched [Transformasi Digital (Digital Transformation Initiative)](transformasi-digital.md) initiative
- **2020** — [Pivot PPE 2020 — COVID-19 Response Case Study](pivot-ppe-2020.md) — pivoted to PPE production during COVID-19
- **2021** — Deployed [Sistem Tenun Otomatis (Automated Weaving System)](mesin-tenun-otomatis.md) across both factories
- **2022** — Formed joint venture with [Toray Industries](toray-industries.md)
- **2023** — Announced expansion to [Surabaya — Expansion Target](surabaya.md) (see [Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md))

## Strategic Priorities (2024-2026)

1. **Expansion:** Establish third production facility in Surabaya
2. **Sustainability:** Achieve 50% renewable energy usage via [Panel Surya 2024 (Solar Panel Project 2024)](panel-surya-2024.md)
3. **Digitalization:** Complete ERP migration and IoT integration
4. **Market Diversification:** Enter Middle East and Australia markets
