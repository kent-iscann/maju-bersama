# Analisis Kinerja Keuangan 2023 (Financial Performance Analysis 2023)

## Overview

This analysis evaluates PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing's financial performance for the 2023 fiscal year, examining revenue growth, profitability, cash flow, and the impact of aggressive capital expenditures approved in [Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md).

**Data Source:** [Laporan Tahunan 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](laporan-tahunan-2023.md) (audited by KPMG Indonesia)

## Financial Summary

| Metric | 2022 | 2023 | Change | Industry Avg |
|--------|------|------|--------|--------------|
| Revenue (IDR B) | 298 | 340 | +14.1% | +8.5% |
| Gross Profit (IDR B) | 89 | 102 | +14.6% | +9.2% |
| Operating Income (IDR B) | 56 | 68 | +21.4% | +12.1% |
| Net Income (IDR B) | 34 | 41 | +20.6% | +15.8% |
| EBITDA (IDR B) | 56 | 68 | +21.4% | +13.5% |

**Assessment:** ✅ Performance exceeds industry average on all metrics

## Cash Flow Analysis ([Sri Wahyuni](sri-wahyuni.md)'s Concern Validated)

| Metric | 2022 | 2023 | Change |
|--------|------|------|--------|
| Operating Cash Flow (IDR B) | 52 | 48 | -7.7% |
| Capex (IDR B) | 35 | 147.5 | +321% |
| Free Cash Flow (IDR B) | 17 | -99.5 | -684% |
| Cash Balance (IDR B) | 45 | 28 | -37.8% |

**Critical Insight:** ⚠️ Free cash flow deeply negative due to [Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md) capex:
- Surabaya expansion: IDR 85B commitment
- Solar installation: IDR 12B
- Automation (Phase 2): IDR 30B
- ERP migration: IDR 3.5B
- Other capex: IDR 17B

**[Sri Wahyuni](sri-wahyuni.md) was right:** Cash flow strain is real. Company burned IDR 17B in free cash flow.

**Warning Signs:**

- ✅ Debt-to-equity still healthy (0.68x, below 1.0x threshold)

## [Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md) Impact Assessment

### Decisions That Improved Performance
1. ✅ **Toray JV (Jan 2023):** +45% revenue growth in technical textiles
2. ✅ **ERP Migration (Apr 2023):** Better inventory management, margin expansion
3. ✅ **Automation Phase 2 (Jul 2023):** 35% productivity gain, margin improvement

### Decisions That Strained Finances
1. ⚠️ **Surabaya Expansion (Jun 2023):** IDR 85B commitment, FCF negative
2. ⚠️ **Dividend Cut (Dec 2023):** Reduced to 25% (from 40%), shareholder discontent
3. ⚠️ **Solar Investment (Mar 2023):** IDR 12B, long payback (4.2 years)

## Financial Health Score: 7.5/10

**Strengths:**
- ✅ Revenue growth (+14.1%) outpacing industry
- ✅ Margin expansion (all margins up)
- ✅ Strong ROE (18.2%) and ROA (12.4%)
- ✅ Healthy debt levels (0.68x D/E)

**Weaknesses:**
- ❌ Negative free cash flow (-IDR 99.5B)
- ❌ Liquidity below industry minimum (1.2x current ratio)
- ❌ Overleveraged for growth ([Sri Wahyuni](sri-wahyuni.md)'s warning came true)

## Recommendations (for 2024)

1. **Cash Preservation:** Delay non-essential capex until FCF turns positive
2. **Banking Diversification:** Add second bank beyond [PT Bank Mandiri (Persero) Tbk](pt-bank-mandiri.md) ([Sri Wahyuni](sri-wahyuni.md)'s suggestion)
3. **Working Capital Optimization:** Reduce inventory days (currently 95, target 75)
4. **Surabaya Phasing:** Consider phased build to preserve cash (vs full IDR 85B upfront)
