# Pewarnaan Ramah Lingkungan (Eco-Friendly Dyeing)

## Overview

Pewarnaan Ramah Lingkungan is PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing's sustainable dyeing initiative, implementing water-efficient and non-toxic dyeing processes across both factories. The program is a core component of the company's [Program Keberlanjutan (Sustainability Program)](program-keberlanjutan.md) (sustainability program).

**Program Details:**
- **Launch:** 2018 (pilot at [Pabrik Cimahi](pabrik-cimahi.md)), full rollout 2020
- **Coverage (2023):** 60% of all dyeing processes (target: 90% by 2026)
- **Investment:** IDR 18 billion (equipment + training + certification)
- **Led by:** [Lina Susanti](lina-susanti.md) (COO), supported by [Heryanto Prasetyo](heryanto-prasetyo.md) (CTO)

## Key Technologies

### 1. Cold Pad Batch Dyeing
- **Water Savings:** 50% vs conventional hot dyeing
- **Energy Savings:** 40% (no heating required)
- **Application:** Reactive dyes on cotton ([Batik Tradisional (Traditional Batik)](batik-tradisional.md), [Pakaian Siap Pakai (Ready-to-Wear Garments)](pakaian-siap-pakai.md))

### 2. Low-Liquor Ratio Dyeing
- **Technology:** Then Airflow dyeing machines (Germany)
- **Liquor Ratio:** 1:3 (vs traditional 1:10)
- **Water Savings:** 70% per batch
- **Application:** Polyester and blends ([Tekstil Teknis (Technical Textiles)](tekstil-teknis.md))

### 3. Natural Dyes (Traditional Segment)
- **Sources:** Indigo (tarum), turmeric, secang wood, jolawe bark
- **Coverage:** 15% of [Batik Tradisional (Traditional Batik)](batik-tradisional.md) production
- **Certification:** GOTS certified organic dyeing process
- **Premium Pricing:** +25% vs synthetic dyes (niche market)

### 4. Closed-Loop Water Recycling
- **System:** Ultrafiltration + reverse osmosis at both factories
- **Recovery Rate:** 85% water recycled
- **Total Savings (2023):** 18 million liters annually
- **[Panel Surya 2024 (Solar Panel Project 2024)](panel-surya-2024.md):** Solar powers water treatment plants

## Environmental Impact (2023 vs 2018 Baseline)

| Metric | 2018 | 2023 | Reduction |
|--------|------|------|-----------|
| Water Consumption (L/meter) | 45 | 28 | -38% |
| Chemical Oxygen Demand (kg/ton) | 120 | 75 | -37.5% |
| Energy Use (kWh/meter) | 3.2 | 2.4 | -25% |
| CO2 Emissions (kg CO2e/meter) | 2.1 | 1.5 | -28.6% |

## Certifications Achieved

- **OEKO-TEX Standard 100** — Free from harmful substances (all products)
- **GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard)** — Organic cotton/ natural dyes
- **bluesign® Approved** — Chemical management system (2023)
- **ISO 14001:2015** — Environmental management ([Pabrik Lembang](pabrik-lembang.md))

## [Program Keberlanjutan (Sustainability Program)](program-keberlanjutan.md) Integration

This dyeing initiative is a cornerstone of the company's sustainability program:
- **Goal 1:** 90% eco-friendly dyeing by 2026
- **Goal 2:** Zero liquid discharge by 2025 ([Pabrik Lembang](pabrik-lembang.md) already achieved)
- **Goal 3:** Carbon neutral dyeing processes by 2030 (with [Panel Surya 2024 (Solar Panel Project 2024)](panel-surya-2024.md))

## Challenges

1. **Higher Costs:** Eco-friendly dyes cost 15-20% more than conventional
2. **Limited Color Range:** Natural dyes have fewer vibrant options
3. **Longer Processing Time:** Cold dyeing takes 12-18 hours vs 4-6 hours hot
4. **Skill Gap:** Operators need retraining ([Key Decisions 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing](keputusan-2023.md) upskilling helps)

## Future Plans (2024-2026)

- **Blockchain Traceability:** Track dye origins and environmental footprint
- **Bio-Dyes R&D:** Develop new natural dye sources (partnership with local universities + [Divisi Riset (R&D Division)](divisi-riset.md))
- **AI Color Matching:** Reduce dye waste through precise formulation
- **Circular Fashion:** Partner with brands for take-back and re-dyeing programs
