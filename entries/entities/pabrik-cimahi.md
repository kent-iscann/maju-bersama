# Pabrik Cimahi

## Overview

Pabrik Cimahi is PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing's original and largest production facility, located in Cimahi district, West Java. Established in 2003, it serves as the company's primary manufacturing hub for traditional textiles and ready-to-wear garments.

**Facility Details:**
- **Location:** Jl. Raya Cimahi No. 123, Cimahi, West Java
- **Established:** June 1, 2003
- **Size:** 15,000 m² (indoor production area)
- **Employees:** ~800 (as of 2023)
- **Production Lines:** 24 weaving lines, 12 garment assembly lines

## Products Manufactured

1. **[[batik-tradisional]]** — Hand-drawn and stamp batik (40% of output)
2. **[[pakaian-siap-pakai]]** — Ready-to-wear garments for export (50% of output)
3. **[[ppe-medis]]** — Medical PPE (10% of output, post-2020)

## Key Features

### Automation
- **2021:** Deployed 80 units of [[mesin-tenun-otomatis]] (Phase 1)
- **2023:** Additional 120 units installed (Phase 2, see [[keputusan-2023]])
- Result: 35% productivity increase, 15% defect reduction

### Sustainability
- **[[panel-surya-2024]]:** 500 kW solar installation completed March 2024
- **[[pewarnaan-ramah-lingkungan]]:** 60% of dyeing processes use eco-friendly methods
- Water recycling system: saves 10 million liters annually

### Quality Certifications
- ISO 9001:2015 (since 2008)
- OEKO-TEX Standard 100 (since 2015)
- GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard) for organic cotton lines
