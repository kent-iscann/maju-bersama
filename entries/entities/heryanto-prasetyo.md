# Heryanto Prasetyo

## Overview

Heryanto Prasetyo is the Chief Technology Officer of PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing, appointed in 2020. He leads the company's digital transformation and technology adoption initiatives, including the [[mesin-tenun-otomatis]] deployment and [[transformasi-digital]] program.

**Personal Details:**
- **Born:** September 8, 1978, in Jakarta, Indonesia
- **Education:**
  - B.Sc. Computer Science, Universitas Indonesia (UI), 2000
  - M.Sc. Industrial Automation, Technical University of Munich, 2006
- **Previous Experience:**
  - Siemens Indonesia, Automation Engineer (2006-2012)
  - PT Astra International, IT Director (2012-2020)

## Role in Company

Heryanto oversees:
- **[[divisi-riset]]** — R&D for new materials and processes
- **IT Infrastructure** — ERP, IoT, cybersecurity
- **Automation Systems** — [[mesin-tenun-otomatis]], robotics integration
- **[[transformasi-digital]]** — Ongoing digital transformation initiative

### Key Projects Led

#### 1. ERP Migration (2023)
Led migration from legacy SAP to Oracle NetSuite (see [[keputusan-2023]]):
- Budget: IDR 3.5 billion
- Timeline: 8 months (April-December 2023)
- Result: Real-time inventory tracking, better [[divisi-ekspor]] integration

#### 2. Automated Weaving System (2021-2023)
Deployed [[mesin-tenun-otomatis]] across both factories:
- Phase 1 (2021): 80 units at [[pabrik-cimahi]]
- Phase 2 (2023): 120 additional units (approved [[keputusan-2023]])
- Result: 35% productivity increase, 15% defect reduction

#### 3. Solar Energy Implementation
Managed [[panel-surya-2024]] project:
- 1.3 MW total capacity (500kW Cimahi + 800kW Lembang)
- Investment: IDR 12 billion
- Expected payback: 4.2 years

## Technology Philosophy

"Automation should augment, not replace. Every machine deployed must come with a training program for operators." — Led to IDR 2.8B upskilling program ([[keputusan-2023]]).
