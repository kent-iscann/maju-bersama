# Pabrik Lembang

## Overview

Pabrik Lembang is PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing's second production facility, specializing in technical textiles for the automotive industry. The facility was established in 2016 with support from the [[toray-industries]] joint venture.

**Facility Details:**
- **Location:** Jl. Lembang KM 18, Lembang, West Java
- **Established:** November 10, 2015 (operational since March 2016)
- **Size:** 22,000 m² (largest facility)
- **Employees:** ~400 (as of 2023)
- **Production Lines:** 16 specialized weaving lines, 8 coating lines

## Products Manufactured

1. **[[tekstil-teknis]]** — Automotive seat fabrics, interior trims (70% of output)
2. **Industrial Textiles** — Conveyor belts, filtration fabrics (20% of output)
3. **[[pakaian-siap-pakai]]** — High-spec uniforms and workwear (10% of output)

## Key Features

### Technology
- **Specialized Equipment:** 16 rapier looms imported from Italy (ITEMA brand)
- **Coating Lines:** 8 lines for PU/PVC coating of technical fabrics
- **[[mesin-tenun-otomatis]]:** 60 units installed Q3 2023 (see [[keputusan-2023]])

### Sustainability
- **[[panel-surya-2024]]:** 800 kW solar installation (completion target: March 2024)
- Zero-liquid-discharge system for coating processes
- 90% of production uses [[pewarnaan-ramah-lingkungan]] methods

### Certifications
- IATF 16949:2016 (Automotive Quality Management)
- ISO 14001:2015 (Environmental Management)
- OEKO-TEX Standard 100 Class II
