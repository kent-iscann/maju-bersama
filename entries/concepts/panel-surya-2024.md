# Panel Surya 2024 (Solar Panel Project 2024)

## Overview

Panel Surya 2024 is PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing's renewable energy initiative, installing solar photovoltaic systems at both production facilities. Approved in [[keputusan-2023]] (March 10, 2023), the project aims to reduce energy costs by 40% and support the company's [[program-keberlanjutan]] (sustainability program).

**Project Details:**
- **Approval:** March 10, 2023 (vote: 4-1, [[ratna-sari]] opposed on labor concerns)
- **Investment:** IDR 12 billion
- **Total Capacity:** 1.3 MW (500kW Cimahi + 800kW Lembang)
- **Financing:** IDR 10B loan from [[pt-bank-mandiri]] (9.25% p.a., green financing rate)
- **Payback Period:** 4.2 years

## Technical Specifications

### Pabrik Cimahi (500 kW)
- **Panels:** 1,250 units (Jinko Solar 400W mono PERC)
- **Inverters:** 5x 100kW Huawei string inverters
- **Mounting:** Roof-mounted (factory roof + carport)
- **Expected Generation:** 750 MWh/year
- **CO2 Reduction:** 580 tons CO2e/year

### Pabrik Lembang (800 kW)
- **Panels:** 2,000 units (Jinko Solar 400W mono PERC)
- **Inverters:** 8x 100kW Huawei string inverters
- **Mounting:** Roof-mounted + ground-mounted (open area)
- **Expected Generation:** 1,200 MWh/year
- **CO2 Reduction:** 920 tons CO2e/year

## [[program-keberlanjutan]] Integration

This project is a cornerstone of the company's sustainability program:
- **Goal 1:** 50% renewable energy by 2026 (currently 38% after project)
- **Goal 2:** Carbon neutral operations by 2030 (72% progress after this project)
- **Goal 3:** Green financing (achieved via [[pt-bank-mandiri]]'s 9.25% green loan)

## [[ratna-sari]]'s Opposition & Mitigation

### Original Concerns (March 2023)
1. **Labor Reallocation:** Workers needed for installation (temp disruption)
2. **Safety Risks:** Roof work, electrical hazards
3. **Job Security:** Fear that automation + solar = job losses

### Mitigation Measures (Agreed in [[keputusan-2023]])
1. **Phased Installation:** During low-production months (Apr-May, Oct-Nov)
2. **Safety Training:** 50 workers certified for solar maintenance (new skills!)
3. **Job Guarantee:** Zero layoffs policy (reconfirmed for solar transition)
4. **Upskilling:** 50 workers transitioned to solar maintenance roles (no layoffs)

**Outcome:** [[ratna-sari]] now supports the project (concerns addressed).

## Integration with [[mesin-tenun-otomatis]]

- **Direct Power:** Solar feeds weaving machines directly (no transmission loss)
- **Battery Storage (Phase 3, 2025):** 500kWh battery for night operations
- **Smart Grid:** IoT sensors balance solar vs grid based on production schedule
