# Pewarnaan Ramah Lingkungan (Eco-Friendly Dyeing)

## Overview

Pewarnaan Ramah Lingkungan is PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing's sustainable dyeing initiative, implementing water-efficient and non-toxic dyeing processes across both factories. The program is a core component of the company's [[program-keberlanjutan]] (sustainability program).

**Program Details:**
- **Launch:** 2018 (pilot at [[pabrik-cimahi]]), full rollout 2020
- **Coverage (2023):** 60% of all dyeing processes (target: 90% by 2026)
- **Investment:** IDR 18 billion (equipment + training + certification)
- **Led by:** [[lina-susanti]] (COO), supported by [[heryanto-prasetyo]] (CTO)

## Key Technologies

### 1. Cold Pad Batch Dyeing
- **Water Savings:** 50% vs conventional hot dyeing
- **Energy Savings:** 40% (no heating required)
- **Application:** Reactive dyes on cotton ([[batik-tradisional]], [[pakaian-siap-pakai]])

### 2. Low-Liquor Ratio Dyeing
- **Technology:** Then Airflow dyeing machines (Germany)
- **Liquor Ratio:** 1:3 (vs traditional 1:10)
- **Water Savings:** 70% per batch
- **Application:** Polyester and blends ([[tekstil-teknis]])

### 3. Natural Dyes (Traditional Segment)
- **Sources:** Indigo (tarum), turmeric, secang wood, jolawe bark
- **Coverage:** 15% of [[batik-tradisional]] production
- **Certification:** GOTS certified organic dyeing process
- **Premium Pricing:** +25% vs synthetic dyes (niche market)

### 4. Closed-Loop Water Recycling
- **System:** Ultrafiltration + reverse osmosis at both factories
- **Recovery Rate:** 85% water recycled
- **Total Savings (2023):** 18 million liters annually
- **[[panel-surya-2024]]:** Solar powers water treatment plants

## Certifications Achieved

- **OEKO-TEX Standard 100** — Free from harmful substances (all products)
- **GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard)** — Organic cotton/ natural dyes
- **bluesign® Approved** — Chemical management system (2023)
- **ISO 14001:2015** — Environmental management ([[pabrik-lembang]])

## [[program-keberlanjutan]] Integration

This dyeing initiative is a cornerstone of the company's sustainability program:
- **Goal 1:** 90% eco-friendly dyeing by 2026
- **Goal 2:** Zero liquid discharge by 2025 ([[pabrik-lembang]] already achieved)
- **Goal 3:** Carbon neutral dyeing processes by 2030 (with [[panel-surya-2024]])

## Challenges

1. **Higher Costs:** Eco-friendly dyes cost 15-20% more than conventional
2. **Limited Color Range:** Natural dyes have fewer vibrant options
3. **Longer Processing Time:** Cold dyeing takes 12-18 hours vs 4-6 hours hot
4. **Skill Gap:** Operators need retraining ([[keputusan-2023]] upskilling helps)

## Future Plans (2024-2026)

- **Blockchain Traceability:** Track dye origins and environmental footprint
- **Bio-Dyes R&D:** Develop new natural dye sources (partnership with local universities + [[divisi-riset]])
- **AI Color Matching:** Reduce dye waste through precise formulation
- **Circular Fashion:** Partner with brands for take-back and re-dyeing programs
