# Sistem Tenun Otomatis (Automated Weaving System)

## Overview

The Sistem Tenun Otomatis (Automated Weaving System) is PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing's flagship automation initiative, deploying computerized rapier looms across both production facilities. The system was implemented in two phases (2021 and 2023) under the leadership of [[heryanto-prasetyo]] (CTO) and [[ahmad-darmawan]] (Production Director).

**System Details:**
- **Technology:** ITEMA R9000 rapier looms (Italy)
- **Control System:** Siemens PLC with IoT connectivity
- **Total Units:** 260 looms (80 Cimahi 2021 + 120 Cimahi 2023 + 60 Lembang 2023)
- **Investment:** IDR 78 billion total (phases 1 & 2)

## Technical Specifications

| Parameter | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Weaving Width | 1900mm - 3600mm (adjustable) |
| Speed | 650-850 rpm (depending on fabric type) |
| Yarn Types | Cotton, Polyester, Blends, Technical fibers |
| IoT Sensors | 12 per machine (vibration, temp, tension, picks/cm) |
| Connectivity | OPC-UA to Oracle NetSuite ERP |

## Implementation Timeline

### Phase 1 (2021) — Cimahi Factory
- **Units:** 80 looms
- **Decision:** [[keputusan-2023]] (ratified retroactively, original approval 2020)
- **Results:** 35% productivity increase, 15% defect reduction
- **Lessons Learned:** Need comprehensive training program (led to [[keputusan-2023]] upskilling budget)

### Phase 2 (2023) — Expansion
- **Units:** 180 additional looms (120 Cimahi + 60 Lembang)
- **Decision:** July 15, 2023 (see [[keputusan-2023]])
- **Results:** Full automation of 90% of weaving capacity
- **Upskilling:** 500 workers trained ([[ratna-sari]] led program)

## Performance Metrics (2023 vs 2022)

- **Productivity:** +35% (meters produced per operator hour)
- **Defect Rate:** 3.4% → 2.1%
- **Downtime:** Reduced 40% (predictive maintenance via IoT)
- **Energy Efficiency:** 18% reduction per meter produced
- **Labor Impact:** Zero layoffs; 500 workers upskilled to technical roles

## Sustainability Impact

- Integrated with [[panel-surya-2024]] — solar power directly feeds weaving lines
- Reduced waste: 12% less fabric waste due to precision control
- [[pewarnaan-ramah-lingkungan]] compatibility: automated dye lot tracking

## Challenges & Solutions

### Challenge 1: Worker Resistance
- **Issue:** Fear of job losses ([[serikat-pekerja]] concerns)
- **Solution:** [[keputusan-2023]] upskilling program; zero layoff guarantee

### Challenge 2: Technical Training
- **Issue:** Lack of technicians familiar with Italian machinery
- **Solution:** ITEMA sent 3 trainers for 6 months; [[heryanto-prasetyo]] established in-house certification

### Challenge 3: Integration with Legacy Systems
- **Issue:** Old ERP (SAP) couldn't handle real-time IoT data
- **Solution:** Accelerated ERP migration to Oracle NetSuite ([[keputusan-2023]])

## Future Plans (2024-2025)

- **Phase 3:** Additional 100 looms for [[surabaya]] factory
- **AI Integration:** Computer vision for real-time defect detection (pilot Q3 2024)
- **Digital Twin:** Virtual replica for simulation and optimization (R&D with [[toray-industries]])
