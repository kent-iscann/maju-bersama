# Tekstil Teknis (Technical Textiles)

## Overview

Tekstil Teknis is PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing's highest-growth product line, producing specialized fabrics for automotive and industrial applications. The line operates primarily at [[pabrik-lembang]] under the joint venture with [[toray-industries]].

**Product Details:**
- **Revenue (2023):** IDR 102 billion (30% of total revenue)
- **Growth:** +45% YoY (boosted by Toray JV)
- **Production:** 1.2 million meters/year
- **Markets:** Domestic automotive (70%), Export via Toray (30%)
- **Facility:** [[pabrik-lembang]] (specialized coating lines)

## Product Categories

### Automotive Textiles (70% of technical line)
1. **Seat Fabrics** — Toyota, Honda, Daihatsu specifications
2. **Interior Trims** — Door panels, headliners, package trays
3. **Safety Components** — Airbag fabrics, seatbelt webbing
4. **Acoustic Textiles** — Sound-dampening materials

### Industrial Textiles (30% of technical line)
1. **Conveyor Belts** — Heavy-duty fabrics for mining/shipyards
2. **Filtration Fabrics** — Air and liquid filtration media
3. **Protective Textiles** — Fire-resistant, chemical-resistant fabrics
4. **Geotextiles** — Infrastructure/construction applications

## Technology & Processes

### Manufacturing
- **Looms:** 16 ITEMA rapier looms (same as [[mesin-tenun-otomatis]] but specialized settings)
- **Coating Lines:** 8 lines for PU/PVC coating (fire retardant, water repellent)
- **Testing Lab:** On-site tensile, abrasion, flammability testing (IATF 16949 certified)

### Technology Transfer from [[toray-industries]]
- Advanced fiber blending (polyester/nylon/aramid hybrids)
- Japanese quality control standards (ppm-level defect tracking)
- R&D collaboration with [[divisi-riset]] for new material development

## Certifications

- **IATF 16949:2016** — Automotive quality management (mandatory for Toyota/Honda)
- **ISO 9001:2015** — General quality management
- **OEKO-TEX Standard 100 Class II** — Harmless for human contact
- **ISO 3795** — Flammability testing for automotive interiors

## Competitive Advantages

1. **Toray Partnership:** Access to cutting-edge Japanese fiber technology
2. **IATF Certification:** Only 12 Indonesian textile companies certified (2023)
3. **Geographic Proximity:** Lembang factory 3 hours from major auto plants
4. **Integrated Production:** Weaving to coating in single facility

## Challenges

1. **Skilled Labor Shortage:** Difficulty hiring technicians ([[ahmad-darmawan]]'s challenge)
2. **Import Competition:** Chinese technical textiles at lower price points
3. **Raw Material Costs:** Specialized fibers up 18% in 2023
4. **Capital Intensity:** Coating lines require IDR 15B+ investment per line

## Future Plans (2024-2026)

- **Capacity Expansion:** 4 additional coating lines (IDR 60B, funded by Surabaya expansion budget [[keputusan-2023]])
- **New Materials:** Carbon fiber textiles for premium automotive (R&D with [[toray-industries]])
- **Export Growth:** Target 50% export ratio by 2026 (currently 30%)

## Sustainability Integration

- **[[pewarnaan-ramah-lingkungan]]:** 90% of dyeing uses eco-friendly processes
- **[[panel-surya-2024]]:** 800kW solar at [[pabrik-lembang]] (completion March 2024)
- **Zero Liquid Discharge:** Closed-loop water system for coating processes
