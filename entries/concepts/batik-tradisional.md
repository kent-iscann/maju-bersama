# Batik Tradisional (Traditional Batik)

## Overview

Batik Tradisional is PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing's heritage product line, producing authentic Indonesian batik textiles using both traditional hand-drawn (batik tulis) and modern assisted techniques. This product line represents the company's roots, dating back to its founding in 1998 by [[budiarjo-widodo]].

**Product Details:**
- **Revenue (2023):** IDR 85 billion (25% of total revenue)
- **Production:** 800,000 meters/year
- **Markets:** Domestic (60%), Japan (25%), USA/EU (15%)
- **Facility:** Primarily [[pabrik-cimahi]] ([[pabrik-lembang]] produces minimal batik)

## Production Techniques

### Batik Tulis (Hand-Drawn)
- **Artisans:** 120 skilled craftspeople
- **Process:** Hot wax applied with canting tool, dip-dyeing, wax removal
- **Output:** ~15,000 meters/year (premium segment, IDR 150k-450k/meter)
- **Certification:** Government-recognized "Batik Asli Indonesia" seal

### Batik Cap (Stamp-Assisted)
- **Process:** Copper stamp (cap) for wax application, machine dyeing
- **Output:** ~785,000 meters/year (mass market, IDR 35k-120k/meter)
- **Automation:** [[mesin-tenun-otomatis]] for base fabric; hand-finishing

### [[cetak-batik-digital]] (Digital Print)
- **Launched:** 2021
- **Process:** Digital printing on cotton/silk base fabrics
- **Output:** ~100,000 meters/year (custom designs, IDR 45k-200k/meter)
- **Growth:** +85% in 2023 (fastest-growing batik subsegment)

## Design Categories

1. **Parang Series** — Royal patterns from Yogyakarta (premium line)
2. **Kawung Collection** — Geometric designs, popular in domestic market
3. **Modern Fusion** — Contemporary interpretations for younger demographics
4. **Custom Corporate** — Branded batik for company uniforms ([[divisi-ekspor]])

## Sustainability Practices

- **[[pewarnaan-ramah-lingkungan]]:** 80% of production uses natural or eco-friendly dyes
- **Water Recycling:** Closed-loop system at [[pabrik-cimahi]] saves 5M liters/year
- **Artisan Welfare:** Fair trade certified; artisans earn 2.5x minimum wage
- **[[program-keberlanjutan]]:** Part of company's broader sustainability initiative

## Challenges

1. **Counterfeit Products:** Cheap imports from China affecting domestic market
2. **Artisan Shortage:** Young people less interested in traditional crafts
3. **Rising Costs:** Cotton prices up 22% in 2023 (see [[laporan-tahunan-2023]])
