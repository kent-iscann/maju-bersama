# Laporan Tahunan 2023 — PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing

## Overview

PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing's 2023 fiscal year marked the company's most aggressive expansion year since its founding in 1998. Under the leadership of [[budiarjo-widodo]], the company committed IDR 147.5 billion in capital expenditures while maintaining profitability.

## Financial Performance

| Metric | 2022 | 2023 | Change |
|--------|------|------|--------|
| Revenue (IDR billion) | 298 | 340 | +14.1% |
| Gross Profit (IDR billion) | 89 | 102 | +14.6% |
| Net Income (IDR billion) | 34 | 41 | +20.6% |
| EBITDA (IDR billion) | 56 | 68 | +21.4% |
| Employees | 1,150 | 1,200 | +4.3% |

## Revenue Breakdown by Segment

1. **Traditional Textiles ([[batik-tradisional]])** — IDR 85B (25%)
2. **Ready-to-Wear ([[pakaian-siap-pakai]])** — IDR 136B (40%)
3. **Technical Textiles ([[tekstil-teknis]])** — IDR 102B (30%)
4. **PPE Products ([[ppe-medis]])** — IDR 17B (5%) *[down from 15% in 2022]*

## Market Performance

### Export Markets
- **Japan:** IDR 119B (35%) — [[toray-industries]] partnership boosted technical textiles
- **USA:** IDR 85B (25%) — steady demand for ready-to-wear
- **EU:** IDR 68B (20%) — growth in sustainable textile demand
- **Others:** IDR 68B (20%) — includes new Middle East initiatives

### Domestic Market
- Revenue: IDR 68B (20% of total)
- Key customers: Government uniform contracts, retail brands

## Operational Highlights

### Production
- **Fabric produced:** 2.8 million meters (+12% YoY)
- **Garments produced:** 1.2 million units (+8% YoY)
- **Defect rate:** 2.1% (improved from 3.4% in 2022 due to [[mesin-tenun-otomatis]])

### Sustainability
- Solar installation at [[pabrik-cimahi]] completed Q4 (see [[panel-surya-2024]])
- [[pewarnaan-ramah-lingkungan]] adoption reached 60% of production lines
- Water recycling system saved 18 million liters annually

## Key Challenges

1. **Rising raw material costs** — Cotton prices up 22% in 2023
2. **Labor shortages** — Difficulty hiring skilled technicians for [[pabrik-lembang]]
3. **Cash flow pressure** — Aggressive capex ([[keputusan-2023]]) strained liquidity
4. **Supply chain disruptions** — Red Sea crisis affected European deliveries Q4

## Outlook 2024

- **Revenue target:** IDR 400 billion (+17.6%)
- **Surabaya factory ([[surabaya]])** operational Q4 2024
- **Solar expansion** to [[pabrik-lembang]] by March 2024
- **New markets:** UAE, Saudi Arabia, Australia ([[divisi-ekspor]] leading)
