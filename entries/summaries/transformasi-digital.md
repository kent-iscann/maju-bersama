# Transformasi Digital (Digital Transformation Initiative)

## Overview

Launched on February 28, 2018 by [[budiarjo-widodo]] (CEO), the Transformasi Digital initiative aimed to modernize PT Maju Bersama Manufacturing's operations through automation, IoT integration, and cloud-based systems. Led by [[heryanto-prasetyo]] (CTO), the initiative spanned 2018-2023 with mixed results.

**Budget:** IDR 25 billion (2018-2023 cumulative)
**Scope:** ERP migration, IoT deployment, e-commerce integration, data analytics

## Key Milestones

### Phase 1 (2018-2020): Foundation
- **ERP Selection:** Chose Oracle NetSuite over SAP S/4HANA
- **Infrastructure:** Upgraded factory networking (WiFi 6, fiber backbone)
- **Training:** 200 staff trained on digital tools
- **Investment:** IDR 8B

### Phase 2 (2021-2022): Automation
- **[[mesin-tenun-otomatis]]:** 80 looms deployed at [[pabrik-cimahi]] (Phase 1)
- **IoT Sensors:** 960 sensors installed (12 per machine)
- **Predictive Maintenance:** Reduced downtime 25%
- **Investment:** IDR 12B

### Phase 3 (2023): Integration
- **ERP Migration:** Completed December 2023 (see [[keputusan-2023]])
- **Oracle NetSuite Go-Live:** Real-time inventory tracking
- **[[divisi-ekspor]] Integration:** Direct API with major customers
- **Investment:** IDR 5B (ERP) + IDR 30B (Automation Phase 2) = IDR 35B

## Major Achievements

### 1. ERP Migration ([[keputusan-2023]])
- **From:** Legacy SAP (2008-era, heavily customized)
- **To:** Oracle NetSuite (cloud-native, API-first)
- **Timeline:** 8 months (April-December 2023)
- **Cost:** IDR 3.5B (under budget by IDR 500M)
- **Result:** ✅ Real-time inventory, automated ordering, [[divisi-ekspor]] integration

### 2. Automated Weaving ([[mesin-tenun-otomatis]])
- **Productivity:** +35% (meters per operator hour)
- **Defects:** 3.4% → 2.1% (15% reduction)
- **Downtime:** -40% (predictive maintenance via IoT)
- **ROI:** 22% IRR (well above 15% hurdle rate)

### 3. [[cetak-batik-digital]] Integration
- **Digital Printing:** 6 Epson printers connected to NetSuite
- **Custom Orders:** API link for direct-to-consumer ([[strategi-omni-channel]])
- **Growth:** +85% YoY (2023), fastest-growing product segment

## Challenges & Failures

### 1. Change Management (2018-2019)
- **Issue:** 30% staff resistance to digital tools
- **Impact:** Delayed Phase 1 by 6 months
- **Solution:** [[ratna-sari]]'s change management program + incentives
- **Lesson:** Technology without people readiness = waste

### 2. Legacy System Integration (2020-2021)
- **Issue:** Legacy SAP couldn't handle IoT data ([[mesin-tenun-otomatis]])
- **Impact:** Forced early ERP migration (originally planned for 2022)
- **Solution:** Accelerated Oracle NetSuite project ([[keputusan-2023]])
- **Lesson:** Plan for interdependencies; don't assume systems will "just work"

### 3. [[strategi-omni-channel]] Slow Adoption (2022-2023)
- **Issue:** Only 5% of export sales via direct channels (target: 20%)
- **Impact:** IDR 2B investment in e-commerce platform underutilized
- **Solution:** Partnership with Tokopedia, Shopee (domestic), Alibaba (export)
- **Lesson:** Platform strategy > building own e-commerce (for now)

## Financial Impact (2018-2023)

| Metric | 2018 (Pre-Digital) | 2023 (Post-Digital) | Change |
|--------|---------------------|----------------------|--------|
| Revenue (IDR B) | 245 | 340 | +38.8% |
| Gross Margin | 28.5% | 30.0% | +1.5pp |
| Defect Rate | 3.6% | 2.1% | -41.7% |
| Inventory Turns | 4.2x | 6.8x | +61.9% |
| Overtime Costs | IDR 8B | IDR 4.5B | -43.8% |

**ROI (5-year):** 18.5% IRR (above 15% hurdle rate ✅)

## Strategic Assessment: 7.5/10

**Strengths:**
- ✅ ERP migration successful ([[keputusan-2023]])
- ✅ [[mesin-tenun-otomatis]] delivering on promises
- ✅ Measurable efficiency gains (inventory, defects, overtime)
- ✅ Strong ROI (18.5%)

**Weaknesses:**
- ❌ [[strategi-omni-channel]] underperformed (5% vs 20% target)
- ❌ Change management underestimated (6-month delay)
- ❌ Legacy integration issues (forced early ERP migration)
- ⚠️ \([[sri-wahyuni]]'s concern: IDR 147.5B total capex in 2023 strained cash)

## 2024-2026 Roadmap

1. **AI Integration:** Computer vision for defect detection (pilot Q3 2024)
2. **Digital Twin:** Virtual factory replica ([[divisi-riset]] + [[toray-industries]])
3. **Blockchain:** Supply chain traceability (sustainability claims)
4. **Full [[strategi-omni-channel]]:** Target 15% direct sales by 2026
